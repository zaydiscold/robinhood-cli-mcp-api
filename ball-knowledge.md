## What this file is

A growing basket of broad, finance-relevant context the operator wants the agent to keep in mind:
alpha, thesis fragments, hot sectors, tickers, market hunches, rumors, source leads, X/Twitter
accounts, analysts/newsletters, earnings & investor-day notes, macro, catalysts, trading-style notes,
dividend/income preferences, risk appetite, watchlist ideas — or anything else intentionally added.

An entry can be as small as a ticker, a sector word, or a single `@handle`, or as loose as a hunch or
a rumor. It does not need to be polished, verified, or immediately actionable.

## How to read it (the rules live in SKILL.md "Ball Knowledge")

- **Anything in this file was intentionally added — treat it as important investing context.** That
  means *pay attention to it*, not *blindly obey it*.
- **Context, not proof, not permission.** Nothing here authorizes a trade, proves a rumor, or overrides
  verification, user confirmation, live market data, or brokerage order history.
- **Classify by type before using it** (the file stays unlabeled — the agent infers): rumor → verify;
  bare sector/ticker → keep on the radar; `@handle`/source → a lead, not truth; "0DTE"/leverage → a
  high-risk style note (surface the risk, don't normalize it); "dividend/QDTE" → an income preference.
- **Minor recency bias only.** Newer entries are slightly more relevant; older entries still matter
  unless contradicted, marked obsolete, clearly stale, or removed. This is a timeline, not a feed.
- **Append-only.** New entries go at the bottom in the format below, in date order. Don't rewrite or
  delete older entries unless the owner explicitly asks.

> Public + committed by design — keep entries **generic** here. Sensitive personal alpha, specific
> trusted handles, or anything you don't want public is the owner's discretion (or a future private
> overlay), not the committed seed.
